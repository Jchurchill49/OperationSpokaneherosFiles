The idea for Java for Joes came from Cory Pape, Location General Manager for Schwan’s Home Service, Inc. and former Marine. Cory asked himself, how do I give something to the troops overseas, and also something back to their families in the Greater Spokane Area? Cory remembered the common theme when his buddies returned from deployment, “it is great to have a ‘GOOD’ cup of coffee again.” Java for Joes was born, and Operation Spokane Heroes stepped up to answer the call to meet Cory’s vision.

The goal of Java for Joes is to sell 125,160 bags of Schwan's® Gourmet Coffee. Every bag of coffee will be shipped and distributed to deployed troops in Iraq and Afghanistan. When this goal is met, Schwan's® Home Service, Inc. will donate $200,000 to Operation Spokane Heroes to use to support the local families of the armed services in the Greater Spokane Area.

Money for the purchase of the coffee will come from donations and promotions with local businesses.

Join this GREAT cause, show your support for those protecting us abroad, and help us give back to those families left behind while their loved ones protect our Great Country!
